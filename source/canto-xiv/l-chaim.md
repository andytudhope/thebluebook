# L'Chaim

The closer you travel to
light,
the longer time feels.

Still, the message
is the same:
couple with longer cycles
by moving faster.

Time, speed and distance
were always just
another trinity.

Sing, like the sea
and all the rainbows
she covered herself with
just yesterday.
